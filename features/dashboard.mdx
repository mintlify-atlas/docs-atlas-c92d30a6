The dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. It gives you a real-time snapshot of your carbon footprint for the day and the past week.

## Overview

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Emissions ring" icon="circle-dot">
    A circular gauge showing today's total CO2e in kilograms, updated every time you log an activity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tip del dia" icon="leaf">
    A personalized environmental tip based on your current emission level, refreshed each day.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tendencia semanal" icon="chart-bar">
    A 7-day bar chart (L M X J V S D) showing your daily emissions from Monday through Sunday.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Actividad reciente" icon="list">
    Your last 5 logged activities with name, timestamp, emissions in kg, and a food or transport badge.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Greeting and time context

The header greeting adapts to the time of day:

| Time range | Greeting shown |
|---|---|
| 00:00 – 11:59 | Buenos dias |
| 12:00 – 17:59 | Buenas tardes |
| 18:00 – 23:59 | Buenas noches |

## Emissions ring

The **EmissionsRing** component at the top of the dashboard displays your cumulative CO2e for the current calendar day in kilograms. Below the ring you will see a percentage change relative to yesterday, accompanied by a trending-down icon when you are improving.

<Note>
  The ring resets to 0.00 kg at midnight each day. Emissions are calculated from all food and transport activities logged that day.
</Note>

## Tip del dia

The **Tip del dia** card surfaces a contextual environmental tip. The tip content is selected based on your current emission level — lower emissions unlock different tips than higher ones. Tips rotate daily.

<Tip>
  Keep your daily emissions low to unlock positive reinforcement tips. Higher emission days surface tips focused on reduction strategies.
</Tip>

## Tendencia semanal (weekly chart)

The **Tendencia semanal** section displays a bar chart with one bar per day of the current week. Days are labelled with their Spanish abbreviations:

| Label | Day |
|---|---|
| L | Lunes (Monday) |
| M | Martes (Tuesday) |
| X | Miércoles (Wednesday) |
| J | Jueves (Thursday) |
| V | Viernes (Friday) |
| S | Sábado (Saturday) |
| D | Domingo (Sunday) |

The chart uses the last 7 days of logged data. Days with no logged activities show a zero-height bar.

## Actividad reciente

The **Actividad reciente** list shows your five most recently logged entries. Each row includes:

- **Name** — the food item or transport mode
- **Timestamp** — the date and time of logging
- **Emissions** — the calculated value in kg CO2e
- **Type badge** — color-coded label indicating `food` or `transport`

To see your full history, navigate to [Historial](/features/history).

## Logout

A logout button is available in the top-right corner of the dashboard. Tapping it ends your session and returns you to the login screen.
