## What is Carbon Tracker?

Carbon Tracker is a mobile-first web application that lets you log everyday activities — what you eat and how you travel — and instantly see their CO2 equivalent emissions. By making your carbon impact visible, Carbon Tracker helps you make greener choices day by day.

## Key features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Daily emissions dashboard">
    A visual ring indicator shows your total CO2 equivalent emissions for the current day. Compare against yesterday and spot your weekly trend with a 7-day bar chart.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Food carbon tracking">
    Choose from 35+ food items — from beef and cheese to lentils and bananas — enter the quantity in grams, and Carbon Tracker calculates kg CO2e using peer-reviewed emission factors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Transport tracking">
    Log any trip by selecting a transport mode (car, bus, motorcycle, taxi, walking, cycling) and entering the distance in kilometres. Zero-emission modes are recognised automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Social weekly rankings">
    Share your unique friend code, add friends, and compete on a weekly leaderboard. Lower emissions rank higher. The leaderboard resets every Monday.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Personalised eco-tips">
    Receive a daily environmental tip calibrated to your current emission level — motivational tips when you're doing well, and targeted food/transport advice when emissions are high.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Full activity history">
    Every logged activity is stored in Supabase. Browse your complete history, filtered by day, to review past behaviour.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Register with your name, email, and password. A confirmation email is sent via Supabase Auth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log activities">
    From the dashboard, tap **Alimentos** to log a meal or **Transporte** to log a trip. Each entry is saved to your activity history instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your progress">
    The dashboard shows today's total emissions, a comparison to yesterday, and a 7-day weekly chart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Challenge friends">
    Share your friend code and add others. The weekly ranking shows who in your group emits the least CO2.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tech stack

Carbon Tracker is built on modern, open-source technologies:

| Layer | Technology |
|---|---|
| Framework | Next.js 16 (App Router) |
| Language | TypeScript 5.7 |
| Styling | Tailwind CSS v4 |
| UI components | Radix UI + shadcn/ui |
| Backend & Auth | Supabase (PostgreSQL + Auth) |
| Charts | Recharts |
| Form validation | React Hook Form + Zod |
